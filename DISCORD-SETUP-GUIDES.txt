                🤖 GUIDE 1: CREATE DISCORD BOT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Open this URL: https://discord.com/developers/applications

2. Click "New Application" button
   - Name: "Homelab Manager" (or your choice)
   - Click "Create"

3. Go to "Bot" tab (left sidebar)
   - Click "Add Bot" → "Yes, do it!"
   - Click "Reset Token" → "Yes, do it!"
   - **COPY THE TOKEN AND SAVE IT** ⚠️ Important!

4. Scroll down to "Privileged Gateway Intents"
   - ✅ Turn ON "MESSAGE CONTENT INTENT"
   - ✅ Turn ON "SERVER MEMBERS INTENT"
   - Click "Save Changes"

5. Go to "OAuth2" → "URL Generator" (left sidebar)
   - Under SCOPES, check:
     ✅ bot
     ✅ applications.commands
   - Under BOT PERMISSIONS, check:
     ✅ Send Messages
     ✅ Embed Links
     ✅ Read Message History
     ✅ Use Slash Commands

6. Copy the "GENERATED URL" at the bottom
   - Open it in a new tab
   - Select your Discord server
   - Click "Authorize"

7. Bot should now appear in your server (offline until we deploy it)
